This Privacy Policy explains how Atomic Media ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information of visitors to our website and clients who engage our services. This policy is framed with reference to India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable data protection practices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, company name, and designation, submitted via contact forms, proposals, or email correspondence.
  • Business information: details shared for the purpose of crafting proposals, strategies, or campaigns (e.g. brand assets, target audience data, business goals).
  • Account & access information: login credentials or access tokens for ad accounts, social media pages, or website/CMS platforms, shared solely for service delivery.
  • Website usage data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, collected through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Payment information: billing details required for invoicing; Atomic Media does not store full card or banking credentials, which are processed via secure third-party payment gateways.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries, prepare proposals, and onboard clients.
  • To deliver contracted services, including campaign management, content creation, and reporting.
  • To process payments and maintain accounting/invoicing records.
  • To communicate updates, performance reports, and relevant marketing communications (with opt-out available at any time).
  • To improve our website, services, and internal processes through aggregated, anonymized analytics.

3. Legal Basis & Consent

We process personal data based on consent provided by the individual (e.g. submitting a form, signing a proposal) or as necessary to perform a contract with the Client. Where required, we will seek explicit consent before processing personal data for purposes beyond service delivery.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not sell personal data to third parties.
  • Data may be shared with trusted third-party tools and platforms used to deliver services (e.g. Meta, Google, HubSpot, Zoho, Make.com, hosting providers), strictly for service execution.
  • Data may be disclosed if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Interns and team members accessing client data are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure, including restricted access controls and secure storage of credentials. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

Personal and business data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal/tax obligations (such as GST and invoicing records), or as agreed with the Client. Data is deleted or anonymized once it is no longer required, unless retention is mandated by law.

7. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and gather analytics on site usage. Users can control cookie preferences through their browser settings; disabling cookies may affect certain website functionalities.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or outdated data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal/contractual retention requirements.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Raise grievances regarding the handling of your personal data, which we will address within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services and website are not directed at individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted on our website with a revised effective date.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, data access, or deletion requests, please reach out via info@atomicmedia.in or at the contact details listed on our official website or proposal documents.
